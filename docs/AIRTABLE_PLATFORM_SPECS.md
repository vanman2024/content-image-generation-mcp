## Implementation Notes

### For Image Generation Server:

1. **Aspect Ratios to Support:**
   - 1:1 (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter)
   - 4:5 (Facebook images)
   - 16:9 (Facebook/LinkedIn/Twitter/YouTube videos)
   - 9:16 (TikTok, Instagram Stories/Reels)

2. **File Size Optimization:**
   - Instagram: Target < 30 MB
   - Twitter: Target < 5 MB (strict)
   - TikTok: Target < 287 MB
   - Facebook: Can go up to 4 GB

3. **Caption Generation Limits:**
   - Twitter: 280 chars max (STRICT)
   - TikTok: 150 chars max
   - Instagram: 2,200 chars (but keep short)
   - LinkedIn: 3,000 chars (professional, detailed)
   - Facebook: 63,206 chars (but keep concise)

4. **Hashtag Strategy:**
   - Instagram: Up to 30 (use 10-15 for engagement)
   - LinkedIn: Max 5 (use 3-5 professional)
   - Twitter: Max 2 (use 1-2, inline)
   - TikTok: Max 3 (trending + niche)
   - Facebook: Max 10 (use 3-5)
